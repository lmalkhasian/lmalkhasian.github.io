About me!

Hi there! I'm Lena (pronounced Lenna). I'm a Master's student at Carnegie Mellon University studying Human-computer interaction. I earned my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of Michigan. GO BLUE!

I'm very interested in user research. I love people, and I love hearing their stories. People never stop surprising me. My goal is to make beautiful things that makes life easier for them to be themselves. Please feel free to reach out and send me an email with any comments, feedback about the site or even just to say hi. I'd love to hear from you!
