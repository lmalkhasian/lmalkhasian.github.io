DayShare Systems

DayShare Systems is a web app and iOS app that helps parents keep up to date with their children while they are in daycare. With this system, daycares can provide parents with transparency into their childrenâ€™s eating, sleeping, and potty schedules and share fun images and videos. I developed this system with my 2 teammates for one of my senior design projects at the University of Michigan.

Role Description

My primary role on the team was to develop an iOS application that would interact with the web app the team was building.

Process

We began developing a web app on a Windows Azure server. When we started talking to users of our system, daycares especially, they showed a lot of interest in a mobile application because uploading media would be relatively time consuming otherwise. The iOS application allows provides a quick and reasonable method of uploading photos and videos for daycares, as well as a convenient means for viewing content for parents.
