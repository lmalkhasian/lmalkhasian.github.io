DJ Recess

DJ Recess is a game on the Microsoft Kinect platform, which is aimed toward children with Autism Spectrum Disorder. I developed this game with 4 other students for one of my senior design classes at the University of Michigan. Our goals were to make a fun game that would provide some therapeutic value to our audience.

Role Description

My primary role on the team was to develop the high-level gesture recognition for the game play.

Process

We began by researching Autism via online materials and interviews with family members of children with Autism. When we finalized that we wanted our game to involve therapy via music teaching, we began developing the game. Once we had valid and working game, we tested it with several autistic children and their families. Given their feedback and suggestions, we made improvements to the design. In the game, players are tasked with recognizing and identifying correctly different pitches and patterns to improve their pitch recognition and recall. Following are screenshots of the game.
