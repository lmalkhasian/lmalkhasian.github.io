About the Project

My capstone project focuses on Eaton Switchgear products. Switchgears are the large gray boxes that manage the power supplies going into buildings. My team and I are team Lumin. Our goal is to create the future of switchgear-human interaction to enhance the safety, reliability and efficiency of power management systems.

Role Description

I designed and finalized our research plan. I also interviewed stakeholders, created a plan for synthesizing all of our data, and then led our research synthesis.
